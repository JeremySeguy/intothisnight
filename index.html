  So, first here is a story: when we went to Las Vegas we had to go through 4 different websites to find what were the events, DJs, special parties for any given day. It was impossible to find this information, crucial to us, in one comprehensive and single place. In short, bouncing between many sites and trying to research what was going on that night, became very frustrating and took time away from us attending some great events.

So, we decided to create Intothisnight.com!
